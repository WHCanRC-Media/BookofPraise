1.
Praise God Most High, you servants of the Lord.
Praise his great name and of his glory sing;
with shouts of joy your thankful praises bring.
Let him by all his people be adored,
acknowledged as their sovereign Lord and King.

2.
Praise God the Father, great in majesty;
praise God the Son, who came to save his own;
praise God the Spirit, sent from heaven’s throne,
that he our guide and comforter might be.
Praise Father, Son, and Spirit, Three in One.
