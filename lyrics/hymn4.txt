1.
We praise you, God the Father, the Creator;
we praise you, God the Son, our Lord and Saviour;
we praise you, God the Spirit, coeternal;
one God almighty.

2.
Trinity holy, Unity unbroken,
let all creation praise you without ceasing,
for you are one in truth and love and splendour,
Rock of salvation.

3.
We praise you, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit;
Lord God eternal, we bow down before you.
Yours be all honour, yours the power and glory,
now and forever.
