1.
Christ has risen! Hallelujah!
He is our victorious Head.
Sing his praises! Hallelujah!
Christ has risen from the dead.
He has conquered sin and Satan.
Where, O death, is now your sting?
Jesus Christ alone is King!
Christ has risen! Hallelujah!
He is our victorious Head.
Christ has risen from the dead.

2.
Christ has risen! He, our Saviour,
freed us from the powers of hell.
We are his! In him forever
we have triumphed over all.
Sun of Righteousness and Glory,
dawning with your healing light,
you have put all gloom to flight!
Christ has risen! Hallelujah!
He is our victorious Head.
Christ has risen from the dead.
