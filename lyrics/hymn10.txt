1.
Now blessed be the Lord our God,
the God of Israel,
for he alone does wondrous works:
his glorious deeds excel;
for he alone does wondrous works:
his glorious deeds excel.

2.
And blessed be his glorious name
through all eternity;
the whole earth let his glory fill:
Amen! So shall it be;
the whole earth let his glory fill:
Amen! So shall it be.
