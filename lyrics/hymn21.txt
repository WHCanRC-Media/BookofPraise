1.
While shepherds watched their flocks by night,
all seated on the ground,
an angel of the Lord came down
and glory shone around.

2.
He said to them, “Be not afraid,
for I have come to tell
good news of great and wondrous joy
for all of Israel.

3.
“To you in David’s town today
was born of David’s line
a Saviour, who is Christ the Lord;
and this will be the sign:

4.
“A newborn Child you there will find,
not as a king arrayed
but humbly wrapped in swaddling cloths
and in a manger laid.”

5.
Then with the angel, all at once,
appeared a mighty throng –
a glorious heavenly multitude,
all praising God in song:

6.
“All glory to our God on high
and on the earth his peace
for men to whom he favour shows
which nevermore shall cease.”
