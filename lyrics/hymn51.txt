1.
Christe, qui lux es et dies Verona, 11th Century
O faithful is this wellknown word:
If we have died with Christ our Lord,
we’ll also live with him again;
his triumph shall not be in vain.

2.
If we endure, so Christ has said,
we’ll also reign with him, our Head.
If we on earth his name betray,
he will disown us on his day.

3.
If we are faithless and we stray,
he faithful will remain for aye,
for he cannot himself deny.
On Christ our Saviour we rely.
