1.
Come, bless the Lord with one accord,
you faithful servants of the Lord,
who praise him in his house by night
and serve him there with all your might.

2.
Your hands in prayer and worship raise;
adore him in his holy place.
Come, kneel before him in his house;
extol his name with sacred vows.

3.
The Lord now bless you from above,
from Zion in his boundless love –
our God who made both earth and sky.
Praise be to him, the Lord Most High.
